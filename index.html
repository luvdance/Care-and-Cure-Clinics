About Us

Care and Cure Clinics is committed to excellence in patient care, offering comprehensive medical services across multiple departments with a focus on compassion, professionalism, and innovation.

Dr. John Ordu

Chief Medical Director, Care and Cure Clinics | MBBS, Medical and Dental Council of Nigeria

Dr. Ordu John, a dedcated medical doctor with nearly a decade of clinical experience, is the founder and Medical Director of Care and Cure Clinics Ltd. His strategic purpose is to bridge the gap between healthcare delivery and data-driven innovation, using digital health technologies to improve patient outcomes and streamline healthcare operations. He has experience leading a private outpatient and emergency clinic, providing care for adult and pediatric populations.

During his career, he has managed outpatient and inpatient care, provided antenatal and gynecologic care, and assisted in numerous surgical procedures. He also has experience in community health outreach and mentoring junior medical staff. Dr. Ordu John's skills include clinical excellence, medical leadership, digital readiness, and emergency triage.
