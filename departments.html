Our Medical Departments

Providing specialized care across various medical fields to serve your health needs.

Cardiology

Our cardiology department is dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of heart-related conditions. We use state-of-the-art technology to provide comprehensive care, from preventive screenings to advanced treatments.

  • Cardiac Stress Testing
  • Echocardiography
  • Hypertension Management
  • Heart Disease Prevention

Pediatrics

We provide compassionate and expert medical care for children from infancy through adolescence. Our pediatric team is committed to the health and well-being of your little ones, offering everything from routine check-ups to specialized care for childhood illnesses.

  • Routine Vaccinations
  • Well-Child Visits
  • Childhood Illnesses & Injuries
  • Developmental Screening

Neurology

Our neurology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the nervous system. We offer advanced diagnostic tools and personalized treatment plans for conditions ranging from migraines to complex neurological diseases.

  • Migraine & Headache Treatment
  • Stroke & Epilepsy Care
  • Memory & Cognitive Disorder Management
  • Nerve Pain Syndromes

Committed to excellence in patient care, compassion, and innovation.
